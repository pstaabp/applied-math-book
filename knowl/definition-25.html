Definition 2.1.7.

Let \(A\) and \(B\) be matrices. The product of \(A\) and \(B\), or \(C=AB\) can be found in the following manner. Element \(c_{ij}\) is the dot product of the \(i\)th row of \(A\) and the \(j\)th column of \(B\text{.}\)
Note: In order for the dot product to be defined, the number columns of \(A\) must equal the number of rows of \(B\text{.}\) If not the product is not defined.
