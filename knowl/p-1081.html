  1. \(\displaystyle |A| \neq 0\)
  2. The inverse matrix, \(A^{-1}\) exists.
  3. For every vector \(\vec{b}\text{,}\) the equation \(A \vec{x}=\vec{b}\) has a unique solution \(\vec{x} = A^{-1} \vec{b}\text{.}\)
  4. The matrix equation \(A \vec{x} = \vec{0}\) has the unique solution \(\vec{x} = \vec{0}\text{.}\)
  5. The columns of \(A\) are linearly independent.
  6. The rows of \(A\) are linearly independent.
  7. The rank of \(A\) is \(n\text{.}\)
  8. The column space of \(A\) is \(\mathbb{R}^n\)
  9. The row space of \(A\) is \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\)
  10. The null space of \(A\) is \(\{\vec{0}\}\) and the nullity is 0.
  11. The reduced row echelon form of \(A\) is \(I\text{,}\) the identity matrix.
