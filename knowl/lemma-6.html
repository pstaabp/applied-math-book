Lemma 2.1.6. Properties of Transposes.

  1. \(\displaystyle (A+B)^{\intercal} = A^{\intercal} + B^{\intercal}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (cA)^{\intercal} = c A^{\intercal}\)
  3. \((A^{\intercal})^{\intercal} = A\text{.}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle (AB)^{\intercal} = B^{\intercal} A^{\intercal}\)
