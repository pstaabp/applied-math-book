Theorem 2.7.8. Laplace Expansion of Determinants.

The determinant of an \(n \times n\) matrix \(T\) can be found by expanding by cofactors on row \(i\) or column \(j\) of \(T\text{.}\) That is
\begin{equation*} |T| = t_{i,1} T_{i,1} + t_{i,2} T_{i,2} + \cdots + t_{i,n} T_{i,n} \end{equation*}
for any \(i\) satisfying \(1 \leq i \leq n\) or
\begin{equation*} |T| = t_{1,j} T_{1,j} + t_{2,j} T_{2,j} + \cdots + t_{n,j} T_{n,j} \end{equation*}
for any \(j\) satisfying \(1 \leq j \leq n\text{.}\)
