\begin{align*} 2x_3 +4(9) \amp = 10,\qquad\quad \text{or} \\ x_3 \amp = -13, \end{align*}
and then substitute into the first equation
\begin{align*} x_1 + 3(-13) - 9 x_4 + 11 (9) \amp = 14 \quad\quad\text{or}\\ x_1 \amp = 9x_4 - 46 \end{align*}
