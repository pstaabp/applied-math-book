Lemma 5.1.4.

If \(\boldsymbol{v}\) is an eigenvector of \(A\) with corresponding eigenvalue \(\lambda\text{,}\) then \(k\boldsymbol{v}\) is also an eigenvector of \(A\) with corresponding eigenvalue \(\lambda\) if \(k \neq 0\text{.}\)
