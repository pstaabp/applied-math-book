If the matrix is size \(1 \times 1\) with its only element of 0, then the statement is true. Assume it has at least two rows. Let \(j\) be the row with all zeros. The operation \(R_i + R_j \rightarrow R_j\) for any \(i \neq j\) results in a matrix with two identical rows, which has a zero determinant from statement 1.
