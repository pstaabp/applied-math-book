In the other two cases as in equations ((1.6.2)) and ((1.6.3)), both sets of lines have the same slope. In the case of ((1.6.2)) the lines have different \(y\)-intercepts, and therefore the lines are parallel and thus there is no solution to the linear system. In the case of ((1.6.3)), both the slope and \(y\)-intercepts are equal, so the lines are equal and thus any point on the line is a solution, and this system has an infinite number of solutions.
