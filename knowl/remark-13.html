Remark 3.5.3.

The projection of any vector \(\vec{u} \in \mathbb{R}^n\) onto a vector \(\vec{v} \in \mathbb{R}^n\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \text{Proj}_{\vec{v}} \vec{u} = \frac{\langle \vec{u},\vec{v} \rangle}{\langle \vec{v}, \vec{v} \rangle} \vec{v} \end{equation*}
and the vector perpendicular to \(\text{Proj}_{\vec{v}}\vec{u}\) that satisfies \(\vec{u} = \text{Proj}_{\vec{v}}\vec{u} + \vec{u}_{\perp}\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \vec{u}_{\perp} = \vec{u} - \text{Proj}_{\vec{v}} \vec{u} = \vec{u} - \frac{ \langle \vec{u}, \vec{v} \rangle}{\langle \vec{v}, \vec{v} \rangle} \vec{v} \end{equation*}
